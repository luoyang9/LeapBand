usage:
> MyMMLChecker input.mml.txt [-v] [-h] [-r path]
> MyMMLChecker input.mml.txt [-v] [-h] [-r path] -o output.wav [-f 44100] [-b 16]
> MyMMLChecker input.mml.txt [-v] [-h] [-r path] -p [-m 1.0]
-v: verbose
-h: Hit Any Key!
-p: Play direct
-m: master volume
-o: Output to file
-f: frequency
-b: bps
-r: resource data path

port 0: FM tone(PM8)
port 1: Sampling tone(SS8)
port 2: Chiptune tone(CT8)
